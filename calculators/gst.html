How to use the Professional GST Calculator?

Calculating the Goods and Services Tax (GST) is essential for any business operating in India. Our professional-grade GST calculator is designed per the latest guidelines from the GST Council of India. Whether you are an entrepreneur, accountant, or consumer, understanding how to calculate GST-exclusive and inclusive amounts is vital for accurate invoicing and expense management.

GST Components in India: CGST, SGST, IGST

CGST

Central GST collected by the Central Government on intra-state supplies.

SGST

State GST collected by the State Government for transactions within the state.

IGST

Integrated GST collected for inter-state supplies (outside your registered state).

GST Calculation Formula

// Adding GST (Exclusive)

GST Amount = (Net Amount * GST Rate) / 100

// Removing GST (Inclusive)

GST Amount = Net Amount - (Net Amount * (100 / (100 + GST Rate)))

Current GST Slabs for 2024-2025

India currently uses a multi-tier tax structure:

  • 5% Rate: Applied to essential items like sugar, oil, spices, and coffee.
  • 12% Rate: Standard rate for computers, processed food, and business services.
  • 18% Rate: Most common rate for services, telecom, and branded clothing.
  • 28% Rate: Luxury items and demerit goods like high-end cars and tobacco.
