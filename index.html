What services does JYJ & CO. offer?

JYJ & CO provides comprehensive chartered accounting services including audit & assurance, taxation (direct and indirect tax planning, GST compliance), accounting & bookkeeping, compliance services, business advisory, corporate finance, internal controls, NBFC consultancy, NRI tax advisory, cost consultancy, and appeals & litigation support.

Why choose JYJ & CO. for audit services?

Our statutory, internal, and risk-based assurance services ensure absolute financial accuracy. With 300+ audits completed and a 100% compliance success rate, we provide established expertise across global sectors including manufacturing, IT, real estate, and international trade.

Do you handle taxation and tax planning?

Yes, our expert taxation services cover both direct and indirect taxes (GST). We provide strategic tax planning, compliance filing, litigation support, and advisory to optimize your tax structure and ensure full adherence to regulations.

Our accounting & bookkeeping services include precise financial record-keeping, payroll management, financial statement preparation, and virtual CFO services to help you maintain the pulse of your business operations.
