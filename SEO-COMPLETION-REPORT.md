## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

- ❌ **Keyword Stuffing**: Don't overuse keywords (max 2.5%)
- ❌ **Duplicate Content**: Each page must be unique
- ❌ **Buying Backlinks**: Only organic, quality links
- ❌ **Ignoring Mobile**: Over 60% traffic is mobile
- ❌ **Slow Page Speed**: Use GTmetrix to check
- ❌ **Broken Links**: Check regularly
- ❌ **Thin Content**: Write 1200+ words
- ❌ **Misleading Titles**: Be accurate and honest
- ❌ **Ignoring Updates**: Update Google when changes occur
- ❌ **No CTA**: Always include call-to-action
