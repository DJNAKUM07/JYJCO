## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Content is King**: Write better content than competitors
2. **User Intent**: Understand what people are actually searching for
3. **E-E-A-T**: Expertise, Experience, Authority, Trustworthiness
4. **Long-tail**: Easy wins with less competition
5. **Internal Linking**: Connect related content
6. **Regular Updates**: Keep content fresh
7. **Mobile First**: Over 60% searches are mobile
8. **Social Signals**: Share content on social media
9. **Reviews**: Encourage and display customer reviews
10. **Speed**: Page speed matters - use GTmetrix.com

## 🎓 SEO Glossary (Quick Reference)

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **SERP** | Search Engine Results Page |
| **CTR** | Click-Through Rate |
| **Backlink** | Link from another site to yours |
| **Indexing** | Google crawling and storing your pages |
| **Keyword Ranking** | Position of your page in search results |
| **Meta Tag** | HTML tag with information about page |
| **Alt Text** | Descriptive text for images |
| **Long-tail** | Multi-word search phrase |
| **Anchor Text** | Text of a hyperlink |
| **Schema Markup** | Structured data for search engines |
| **301 Redirect** | Permanent page redirect |
| **Crawl** | Process of search engine reading website |
